ABOUT US

Income Eco Run is Southeast Asia’s premier eco-run which champions environmental conservation and aims to encourage everyone to play their part in supporting the cause for zero waste.

The 2019 edition will take place on Sunday, 28 April 2019, at the F1 Pit Building, and includes the following categories:

• 21.1km Half Marathon

• 10km / 10km Mixed Team

• Zero Waste 5km Run

• 1.2km Kids Dash

• 800m Kids Dash

Regardless of the distance you wish to run, we would like to encourage you to be a ‘Zero Waste Runner’, and pledge to be eco-friendly! Your race entitlements as a ‘Zero Waste Runner’ will differ from other participants. For example, if you are competing in the 21.1km half marathon, you will not receive a finisher tee or medal, to champion the cause for zero waste.

For Income Eco Run 2019, we will be introducing our first Zero Waste 5km run. All runners in this category will be ‘Zero Waste Runners’ and will receive a light and handy reusable cup in our bid to make hydration stations at this category 100% paper cup-free. ‘Zero Waste Runners’ for other categories - 10km and 21.1km Half Marathon - are also encouraged to use the reusable cup during the race to make a difference to the cause.

Come join in the fun and do your part for a greener environment!
